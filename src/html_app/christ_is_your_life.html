Introduction
  1. Discuss:
    1. Did you have daily quiet times this past week?
    2. Who did you share with and invite?
  2. Discuss the impact of communion on Sunday.

  1. Colossians 3:1-4: Raised to a new life
    1. Raised with Christ in baptism (Colossians 2:12).
    2. The Holy Spirit, received at baptism, gives you the power to set your mind (thoughts) and set your heart (emotions) on things above.
    3. Christ is your life.
  2. Colossians 3:5-11: Put to death
    1. Discuss and be open about your struggles this week.
    2. Discuss any financial challenges — pledge to God, rent, bills, salary, etc...
    3. Discuss practical ways to crucify your sinful nature.
  3. Colossians 3:12-14: Put on — clothe yourself
    1. Compassion
    2. Kindness
    3. Humility
    4. Gentleness
    5. Patience
    6. Forgiveness
  4. Colossians 3:15-24: New attitudes
    1. Peace
    2. Thankful
    3. Wholehearted
    4. Family Interactions (v. 18-21)
    5. Employer/employee relationships (v. 22-24)
    6. Teacher/student relationships (v. 22-24)
